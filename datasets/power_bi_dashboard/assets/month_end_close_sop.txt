MONTH-END CLOSE STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Power BI Reporting Transition — Finance Operations

PROCESS OVERVIEW
This procedure closes the general ledger each month and refreshes the
downstream Power BI reporting dataset used by Finance leadership. The
process owner is the GL Accounting Lead; the process runs on the second
business day after period close.

TASK: SUB-LEDGER RECONCILIATION
Before the books are closed, all sub-ledgers (AP, AR, Fixed Assets) must
be reconciled against the general ledger and any adjusting journal
entries posted. This task is performed daily during the close window,
not just once at period end.

BUSINESS RULE: GL BALANCE RULE
The general ledger must balance to zero before the close is considered
final. (Owner sign-off and the specific balancing checklist are
maintained outside this document and have not yet been captured here.)

KNOWN RISK: LATE CLOSE
A late close delays the Power BI dataset refresh and the downstream
leadership dashboards. (The specific mitigation steps and risk owner
have not yet been captured here.)

NOTE: this document intentionally does not yet describe which system
of record drives the GL close, or how the dataset refresh is
technically triggered — that information is captured separately as
part of the knowledge transfer gap-resolution process.
